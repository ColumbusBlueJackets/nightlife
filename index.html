A Night on the Town

From the Short North to Bridge Park, endless bars, clubs and music venues keep Columbusites entertained all night long.

Short North

If you love art, Short North has art. If you love bars, Short North has bars. If you love food, Short North has food. In short (pun intended), you'll never be bored in the Short North.

Grandview

A walkable “main street” area is filled with boutiques, restaurants and bars while a new development at the Grandview Yard offers an ever-growing selection of shops and other new establishments.

Bridge Park

The brand-new Bridge Park development in Dublin is bursting with dining and entertainment options to fit any mood. Urban Meyer's Pint House and Pins Mechanical are staples of the Columbus' new hangout spot.

Arena District

The Blue Jackets, Crew and Clippers all call the Arena District home for good reason. There's tons of fun to be had around every street corner so don't wear yourself out too fast!

Rooftop Bars

From Lincoln Social in the Short North to Novak's in the Arena District, plenty of rooftop bars in Columbus offer extraordinary cocktails and beer and even more extraordinary views.

Music

Want to see your favorite artist in person? Check out Kemba LIVE!, Nationwide Arena or the Schottenstein Center. Want to discover a new indie band? Head on over to Bastille or Newport Music Hall!

Clubs

If you want to dance, laugh or just chill, tons of clubs around Columbus are within minutes of downtown. The Funny Bone in Easton Town Center also plays host to world-famous comedians and stand-up.
